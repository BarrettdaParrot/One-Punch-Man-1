One Punch Man

One Punch Man is an anime show that follows a man named Saitama who is 25 and is bald. He got his name as it only takes one punch to defeat anyone he comes across.

Along Saitama's journey he comes across a guy named Genos who is 19 and made up of mostly robotic parts and wishes to become stronger than Saitama
